Parameters
[in]nameThe name of the population. Currently unused.
[in]rateThe desired rate at which asteroids appear in the population. Currently relative to the rates of all other populations.
[in]aMin,aMaxThe minimum and maximum semimajor axes allowed in the population.
[in]eAvg,iAvgThe average eccentricity and (absolute value of) inclination of the population.
